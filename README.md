# worldcup-soccer — every World Cup final, mapped

[![Built with Periplum](https://img.shields.io/badge/built_with-Periplum-4da3ff)](https://periplum.js.org)

The men's football (soccer) **World Cup** finals, 1930–2026, plotted at each tournament's
host city and coloured by host continent — a tournament hopping around the globe every four
years.

**[▶ Open the map](https://periplum.github.io/worldcup-soccer/)** — press play to watch it
travel through the decades, or drag the date slider.

Each marker's popup lists the host, the final venue, and the winner. A showcase consumer of
**[Periplum](https://github.com/periplum/periplum)** — just `index.html` + `data.json`.
Refreshed every four years (next entry: the 2026 winner) — at most once a year.
