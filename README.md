# worldcup_fifa — every World Cup final, mapped

The men's **FIFA World Cup** finals, 1930–2026, plotted at each tournament's host city and
coloured by host continent — a tournament hopping around the globe every four years.

**[▶ Open the map](https://periplum.github.io/worldcup_fifa/)** — press play to watch it
travel through the decades, or drag the date slider.

Each marker's popup lists the host, the final venue, and the winner. A showcase consumer of
**[Periplum](https://github.com/periplum/periplum)** — just `index.html` + `data.json`.
Refreshed every four years (next entry: the 2026 winner).
